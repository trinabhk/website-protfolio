/ process

How an engagement runs

Three steps, in order - because crawl health really is a sequence.

step 01

Audit

I crawl the site, pull Search Console data, and produce a prioritized list of the technical issues holding rankings back.

step 02

Fix

I implement the fixes directly on-platform or hand a clear spec to your dev team - status codes, canonicals, redirects, sitemaps, robots rules, and internal links.

step 03

Verify

I validate fixes in Search Console, track affected URLs, and confirm whether indexing, crawlability, or enhancement issues are clearing.
