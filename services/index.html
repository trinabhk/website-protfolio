Fixes that move pages from noindex to ranked.

I focus on the technical layer - the part of SEO that's about code, configuration, and crawl signals rather than guesswork. Here's where I help.

01

Indexing & crawl repair

When pages won't show up in Google, the cause is usually technical. I audit and fix the signals that block indexing - stray noindex tags, Soft 404s on empty or thin pages, misfiring canonicals, and pages walled off in robots.txt.

/ process

How an engagement runs

Three steps, in order - because crawl health really is a sequence.

step 01

Audit

I crawl the site, pull Search Console data, and produce a prioritized list of the technical issues holding rankings back.

step 02

Fix

I implement the fixes directly on-platform or hand a clear spec to your dev team - status codes, canonicals, redirects, sitemaps.

step 03

Verify

We watch the URLs move to Indexed in Search Console and confirm the fix held. Verifiable, not hand-wavy.
